# Stage 1 compliance boundary

This prototype is a coordination and recordkeeping system. It must not be described as a bank, wallet, custodian, licensed investment manager, or NADRA verification provider.

## Allowed now

- Record transfers members make on existing external rails.
- Maintain schedules, obligations, scores, chat, notices and append-only audit/ledger records.
- Compare curated low-risk schemes using dated, sourced, indicative rates.
- Simulate committee investment allocations and liquidation accounting.

## Feature-flagged until licensed providers exist

- Holding pooled money or payment balances.
- Executing or liquidating investments.
- JazzCash/Easypaisa production collection APIs.
- SMS OTP and transactional messaging.
- CNIC OCR, liveness and face matching.
- Claims of NADRA verification.

Every investment surface must display: “Indicative only. Returns not guaranteed. Not investment advice.”
